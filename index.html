Flapjack Fandango Festival!

Set sail for a seafaring celebration like no other! The Flapjack Fandango Festival is a whimsical, nautical-themed event honoring the beloved Cartoon Network show The Marvelous Misadventures of Flapjack. Fans of Flapjack, Captain K'nuckles, and Bubbie are invited to immerse themselves in a day filled with quirky games, sea shanties, and syrup-soaked fun!

When: Saturday, August 9th, 2025 | 11:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Where: Stormalong Harbor Park, 123 Bubbie Bay Lane, Syrup Shoals, ME

Event Highlights

  • Stormalong Treasure Hunt - A kooky scavenger hunt across the park for golden candied doubloons!
  • Voice Actor Panel & Q&A - Hear behind-the-scenes stories from the world of Flapjack.
  • Syrup Slip & Slide Zone - Cool off on our syrup-themed slide (guaranteed not sticky... hopefully).

Special Guests

  • Thurop Van Orman - Creator and voice of Flapjack!
  • Brian Doyle-Murray - The salty voice of Captain K’nuckles himself!

Featured Food Trucks

  • Bubbie's Brine Bites - Seafood snacks with a sugary twist!
  • K'nuckle Sandwiches - Grilled flapjack paninis and salty sea slaw.
  • The Candy Barrel Bus - All-you-can-eat candy buffet inspired by Peppermint Larry's store.
